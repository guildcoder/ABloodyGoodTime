A Bloody Good Warning

This site contains sudden, startling audiovisual moments — we call them “jumps.” They’re ruthless. If loud or sudden images/sounds can affect you (epilepsy, heart conditions, anxiety disorders, PTSD, etc.), seriously consider leaving or toggling the safe mode below.

For everyone else: this is what makes the show fun. Dare to proceed and you might be rewarded with spine-tingling thrills. Dare not — that’s fine too.

  • Jumpscares may occur randomly between 1.5 and 3 minutes.
  • They are fullscreen GIF flashes + sound.
  • Playing audio/video anywhere on the site pauses all jumpscares while playing.
By accepting you acknowledge an awareness of possible physical or psychological reactions and proceed at your own risk.
